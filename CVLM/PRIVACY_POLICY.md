# Privacy Policy for CVLM

**Last Updated:** November 18, 2025

## Introduction

CVLM ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome Extension and API service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
- **Google Account Data**: When you sign in with Google, we collect:
  - Email address
  - Full name
  - Profile picture URL
  - Google User ID (for authentication only)

### 2. User Content
- **CVs (PDFs)**: Uploaded curriculum vitae files
- **Generated Cover Letters**: AI-generated content based on your CV and job offers
- **Job Offer URLs**: Links to job postings you analyze

### 3. Usage Data
- **Timestamps**: When you upload, generate, or download content
- **Error logs**: Technical information to improve service reliability

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:
1. **Provide the Service**: Generate personalized cover letters
2. **Store Your Content**: Save CVs and letters in your personal account
3. **Authenticate**: Verify your identity via Google OAuth
4. **Improve Quality**: Analyze errors and optimize performance

## Data Storage and Security

- **Location**: Data stored on secure servers with encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with user-specific access controls
- **Files**: PDFs stored separately with unique identifiers
- **Retention**: We keep your data until you delete it or close your account

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI / Google Gemini
- Your CV text and job offer content are sent to AI providers to generate letters
- These providers may process data according to their own policies:
  - [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
  - [Google AI Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Google OAuth
- We use Google Sign-In for authentication
- We do NOT access your Gmail, Drive, or other Google services
- [Google OAuth Privacy](https://developers.google.com/identity/protocols/oauth2)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your stored CVs and letters
- **Delete**: Remove any CV or letter from your account
- **Export**: Download your generated letters as PDFs
- **Close Account**: Contact us to permanently delete all your data

## Data Deletion

We will delete all your data within 30 days of your request.
